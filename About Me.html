丹日回凵卞　冊ヨ






Hi! Please call me Mikee because Christian is my older brother's first name as well so it confuses me haha. I am 20 years old and taking AB MultiMedia Arts at Lyceum of the Philippines University Manila. I have always been passionate about the arts ever since I was a little kid, I have been doodling and sketching and drawing as long as I could remember, so when the time came for me to decide on a career, I decided to follow my passion and aspire to become a Graphic Artist/Animator one day





I am currently the President of an Organization: Lyceum Multimedia Arts Society also known as LMAS, a student artist at Tanghalang Batingaw, our university's Theatre Organization, and am currently employed at Fresh Prints, an overseas apparel company. Right now, my college life is very active and at the same time exhausting, but still enjoying the journey along with its challenges!






Frequently asked Questions

I use Digital, Traditional and also use my body and voice as a medium via singing and dancing.

I started drawing/doodling/sketching ever since I was in pre-school. I didn't listen to what the teachers are saying and was really invested in the things that I draw haha.

I get slumps every now and then tbh, I often feel that I'm not "Growing" or my art is improving despite my constant effort and also get discouraged when I see someone better than me, cause I actually have a bit of a competitve side in me haha. But I learned that growth takes time and that I have something unique inside me that no one else can copy. As long as I start being confident in myself, I'll have the energy to move on. :>
